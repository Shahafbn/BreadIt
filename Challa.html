Our Delicious Challah Bread

We want to share with you our family traditional recipe for Challah Bread. Enjoy!

Bread It's Challah Bread

Ingredients

  • 500 grams All Purpose Flour
  • 1 tbsp dry yeast
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 250 ml cup of water
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, yeast, sugar and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add the oil and water and whisk to combine until a dough is formed.
  3. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  4. Place the dough in a covered bowl, let it rise for 1 and half to 2 hours or until doubled.
  5. Divide the dough to 3 equal pieces and roll into ropes.
  6. Shape the 3 pieces to a braid.
  7. Let the braid rise for 30 minutes. Minwhile, heat the oven to 180 degrees celsius.
  8. After 30 minutes, put the Challah in the oven for 35 to 40 minutes, until the Challah's color become golden.
